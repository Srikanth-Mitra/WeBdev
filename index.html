Welcome to Bengaluru Metro wiki page

This is about the Bengaluru metro famously called as the namma metro which started in the year 2011 with 2 reahces now has around total of around 56.1KM length of the 2 lines. Purple line which is from BYPA to KNG and the green line from NGS to SKL. These cover the major part of the city traffic.
